The Soul of the Forest, By the Water

Welcome to Café Forest Floor, a haven nestled just near the historic Chandpole Bridge. We created this space to capture the peaceful, comforting essence of a forest retreat, right here by the beautiful waters of Udaipur. Our mission is simple: to serve exceptional coffee and comforting food in an atmosphere that encourages you to slow down, relax, and savor the moment.

Every cup of coffee and every dish is prepared with the simple intention of delivering warmth and pure quality. Come, step off the concrete path, and onto the cozy forest floor.
